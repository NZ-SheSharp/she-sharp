## Empowering Women in STEM through mentoring

Our mentorship program facilitates supportive relationships between our mentors and mentees. Through sharing knowledge, advice, and encouragement, we help mentees navigate careers, overcome challenges, and achieve interpersonal goals.

# WHAT YOU GET OUT OF A MENTORSHIP PROGRAM

Research shows participating in a mentorship program provides valuable benefits for both mentees and mentors.

85%

Feel more empowered after joining a mentorship program

90%

Experienced improvement of their interpersonal skills

6x more

Mentors are likely to be promoted 

5x more

Mentees with mentors are likely to be promoted

# BENEFITS OF JOINING THE MENTORSHIP PROGRAM



###### Pathway for personal development

Whether you join as a mentor or mentee, this is an additional opportunity for personal and professional development.



###### Increase knowledge share

The mentorship program serves as a platform for promoting collaborative learning and exploring new perspectives together.



###### Supportive community

With our pool of supportive mentors, you'll get professional guidance, provided valuable advice and support along the way.
